CLAUDE AI POWERED
Claude AI scores each passenger 0–100 using 5 weighted signals:

⬛ Account Age (30%) — accounts under 7 days are the #1 fraud signal. Most banned riders create new accounts.
⬛ Community Reports (30%) — other drivers who flagged this rider ID. Type matters: "refused_payment" > "rude".
⬛ Payment Flags + Chargebacks (25%) — a chargeback = real fraud. Even 1 is a strong red flag.
⬛ Payment Type (10%) — prepaid/Cash App = harder to trace = higher risk. Credit = lowest risk.
⬛ Context: Time + Location (5%) — late night pickups in unfamiliar areas elevate the score.

KNOW YOUR RIGHTS
LEGAL RESOURCES
⚖️
RIGHT TO CANCEL UNSAFE RIDES
Platform policy allows cancellation for safety reasons without acceptance rate penalty. ShieldRide documents all safety cancellations to protect your account from retaliation.
💼
RIGHT TO FAIR PAY & RATE TRANSPARENCY
Many cities require platforms to disclose rate changes in advance. Chicago Ordinance §14-12 requires 14-day notice before rate cuts. File a complaint with your city labor department if rates are reduced without notice.
🛡
PROTECTION FROM WRONGFUL DEACTIVATION
Platforms must offer an appeal process before permanent deactivation. Keep records of every incident report against you. ShieldRide tracks deactivation patterns and can provide documentation for appeals.
